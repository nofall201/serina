Speech Emotion Recognition Interface & Analysis Technology

is an AI-powered system that intelligently analyzes speech to detect emotional states in both Indonesian and English. Leveraging advanced deep learning models and real-time acoustic signal processing, SERINA transforms voice data into actionable emotional insights making human computer interaction more natural, responsive, and emotionally aware.

Methodology

Data Augmentation

Applied noise reduction, time-stretching, and pitch shifting to enhance training data

Feature Extraction

We use Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC) to capture the characteristics of human voice perception, including spectral features that are crucial for emotion recognition.

Metrics Evaluation

Model performance was evaluated using precision, recall, F1-score, and accuracy metrics.

Deep Learning Model

SERINA use CNN to train diverse speech datasets to accurately classify multiple emotional states from voice patterns

Privacy Protection

All audio processing happens locally, ensuring your voice data remains private and secure.

Audio Segmentation

Audio was segmented to enable more precise and efficient analysis.
